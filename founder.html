The Story

Engineering Meets Markets

Mohamed Hanas is a software engineer with experience in enterprise integrations, cloud technologies, automation, and financial markets. His technical career spans building systems that connect disparate data sources, automate complex workflows, and solve real operational challenges at scale.

His parallel interest in financial markets grew alongside his engineering career — not as a separate pursuit, but as a natural extension of the same analytical instincts that drive good software engineering: structured thinking, systematic problem-solving, and a preference for data over opinion.

Through Verixa, he combines engineering expertise with market research and technology development to create innovative trading solutions and analytical tools. Every tool Verixa builds reflects the same philosophy: transparent logic, testable behaviour, clean architecture, and continuous improvement.

The Vision

What Verixa Is Building Toward

The long-term vision is straightforward: become a financial technology company that professional traders and institutions trust — not because of aggressive marketing, but because of the consistent quality and integrity of what gets built.

That means expanding the technology platform beyond a single indicator toward a comprehensive suite — analytics dashboards, algorithmic framework libraries, cloud-based market data tools, and AI-assisted research systems.

Every trader who uses a Verixa tool should come away with a clearer understanding of what they're seeing in the market, not more noise or false confidence.
