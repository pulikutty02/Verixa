Overview

Technology-Driven Market Analysis

The Verixa Intelligence Suite brings together multiple analytical frameworks into a single, cohesive TradingView experience — engineered for clarity, not complexity.

TradingView Integration

Runs natively inside your TradingView workspace. No external apps, no browser plugins, no APIs to configure — just add it and it works.

Advanced Market Structure

Automatically identifies and annotates key market structure events — break of structure, change of character, order blocks, and liquidity levels.

User-Friendly Design

Despite its analytical depth, the indicator is designed to present information clearly. Visual hierarchy ensures the most critical data is immediately visible.

Key Features

What the Indicator Does

Market Structure Analysis

Detects and labels Break of Structure (BOS) and Change of Character (CHoCH) events in real time, giving traders a clear structural map of market direction.

Trend Identification

Multi-timeframe trend bias calculation provides a composite view of directional pressure across different time horizons simultaneously.

Order Block Detection

Automatically identifies institutional order blocks and fair value gaps — high-probability reaction zones that institutional price action theory uses as reference points.

Smart Alerts

Configurable alert conditions notify you when key structural events occur — no need to watch charts continuously. Integrate with email, mobile, or webhook.

Data Visualization

Visual layers are carefully designed to add information without cluttering the chart. Clean, professional presentation that fits into a trader's existing workflow.

Continuous Improvement

The indicator evolves with ongoing market research and user feedback. Updates are pushed directly to TradingView — improvements are automatic.

How It Works

Analytical Architecture

The indicator is built around a layered analytical model that processes market data through multiple structured stages.

01

Price Data Ingestion

Raw OHLCV data is consumed from TradingView's data feed, preprocessed, and structured for analysis. The indicator works across all instruments and timeframes available on TradingView.

02

Structure Detection Engine

A rules-based engine processes price data to identify swing highs, swing lows, and structural pivots. These form the foundation for all higher-level analysis.

03

Multi-Timeframe Analysis

Structural data from multiple timeframes is synthesized into a unified bias model. Higher timeframe context anchors the interpretation of lower timeframe signals.

04

Visual Output & Alerts

Results are rendered as clearly labelled visual overlays on the chart, with configurable alert conditions for key structural events. The trader retains full decision-making authority.
