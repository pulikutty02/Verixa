Key Features

What the Indicator Does

Market Structure Analysis

Detects and labels BOS and CHoCH events in real time, giving traders a clear structural map of market direction.

Trend Identification

Multi-timeframe trend bias calculation provides a composite view of directional pressure across different time horizons simultaneously.

Order Block Detection

Automatically identifies institutional order blocks and fair value gaps — high-probability reaction zones used as key reference points.

Smart Alerts

Configurable alert conditions notify you when key structural events occur. Integrate with email, mobile, or webhook — no chart watching required.

Data Visualization

Visual layers are carefully designed to add information without cluttering the chart. Clean, professional presentation that fits existing workflows.

Continuous Improvement

The indicator evolves with ongoing market research and user feedback. Updates pushed directly to TradingView — improvements are automatic.

How It Works

Analytical Architecture

The indicator is built around a layered analytical model that processes market data through multiple structured stages.

01

Price Data Ingestion

Raw OHLCV data is consumed from TradingView's data feed, preprocessed, and structured for analysis. Works across all instruments and timeframes.

02

Structure Detection Engine

A rules-based engine processes price data to identify swing highs, swing lows, and structural pivots — the foundation for all higher-level analysis.

03

Multi-Timeframe Analysis

Structural data from multiple timeframes is synthesized into a unified bias model. Higher timeframe context anchors lower timeframe signal interpretation.

04

Visual Output & Alerts

Results rendered as clearly labelled visual overlays with configurable alert conditions. The trader retains full decision-making authority.
