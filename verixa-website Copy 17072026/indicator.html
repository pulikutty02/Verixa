Verixa 4C Model — V1

Flagship Indicator

The Verixa 4C Model is Verixa Finance's proprietary multi-confirmation trading indicator, developed to identify high-probability Buy and Sell opportunities using a structured four-stage analytical framework. Rather than relying on a single technical condition, the indicator evaluates multiple confirmation layers before presenting a trading opportunity — encouraging disciplined execution and a focus on higher-quality market conditions.

Features
  • Proprietary Four-Step Confirmation Engine
  • Buy & Sell Trade Signals
  • Trend Validation
  • Market Structure Analysis
  • TradingView Alert Support
  • Professional Chart Visualization
  • Clean User Interface
  • Forex, Gold (XAUUSD), Indices & liquid markets
How Traders Use It
  • Identify structured opportunities before committing capital

How It Works

Analytical Architecture

Each indicator in the suite is built around a layered analytical model that processes market data through multiple structured stages.

01

Price Data

Raw OHLCV data is consumed from TradingView's data feed, preprocessed and structured for analysis across all instruments and timeframes.

02

Structure Detection

A rules-based engine processes price data to identify swing highs, swing lows and structural pivots — the foundation for higher-level analysis.

03

Multi-Timeframe Analysis

Structural data from multiple timeframes is synthesized into a unified bias model, anchoring lower timeframe signals to higher timeframe context.

04

Visual Output

Results render as clearly labelled overlays with configurable alerts. The trader retains full decision-making authority at every stage.
